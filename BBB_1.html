Welcome to Baala Bhadra Bhavan

Previous Next

Baala Bhadra Bhavan is a pure vegetarian family restaurant dedicated to serving tasty and traditional South Indian cuisine in a clean and homely environment. With a passion for food and hospitality, our motto is simple yet powerful — “We respect your hungry.”

Located in the culturally rich city of Warangal, we are proud to be near the historic Bhadrakali Temple — one of the oldest temples dedicated to Goddess Bhadrakali, nestled by the serene Bhadrakali Lake. Whether you're here for darshan or just passing through, Baala Bhadra Bhavan is the perfect place to enjoy a satisfying, soulful meal just a few steps away.
